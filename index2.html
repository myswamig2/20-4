Merindilogun Certification Course

On the Mat with Merindilogun — 20-4 (16 Cowrie Shell Divination)

This training introduces Iyawo, priests and priestesses to the sacred art of Merindilogun “20-4” (Sixteen Cowrie Shell Divination). Through daily practice, seniority work, Odu interpretation and diagnostics (Ire/Ibi), the student becomes competent, confident, and spiritually grounded.

Lesson Modules (1–5)

Lesson 1 — Daily Casting Foundations

  • Cast 16 times daily for 16 days.
  • Log date, shell count, seniority, Orisha, 3-word summary.

Lesson 2 — Seniority Determination

  • Cast twice; compare seniority.
  • Senior → (+), Junior → (–).

Lesson 3 — Identifying Ire vs Ibi

  • Create Elder and Junior Odu pairs.
  • Elder senior → Ire; else Ibi.

Lesson 4 — Dot Marking System

  • Mark first pair as Ire/Ibi based on second.
  • Use single/double dot notation.

Lesson 5 — Sources of Ire/Ibi (Igbo Method)

  • Igbo-Ire for up-cowries; Igbo-Ibi for down-cowries.
  • Ibi → check Iku, Ofo, Ija, Oran.
  • Ire → Aiku, Aje, Rin, Omo.

Course Flow

  • 8–10 week foundations track.
  • Weekly log submissions.
  • Certificate upon successful practicum.
