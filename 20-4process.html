Single Cast Method

Eldest

The closest four elements establish the Eldest Odu based on orientation and direction.

Respect of Eldership

The process asks whether the Eldership of the Eldest is respected by Elders, Seniors, or Juniors.

Transformation

If support appears at a later level, the Eldest in Ibi may be transformed into Ire by that Odu.
